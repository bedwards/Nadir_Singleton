The watchman with a lantern, the watchman with a cane
He walks the little alley and he whistles in the lane
A little bit of Sousa and a little bit of hymn
The evening is a friend of him

The watchman with a pocket and a watch upon a chain
He meets a little fellow on the way to the train
A little bit of weather and a little bit of news
The evening is a pair of shoes

Honey, the watchman is a patient little tune
Honey, he follows every window and every moon
Honey, he nods at every door on his patient little row
Honey, we hum along as we go
