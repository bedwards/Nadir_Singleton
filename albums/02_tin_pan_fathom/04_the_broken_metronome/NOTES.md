# The Broken Metronome — notes

AABA in D major, 100 bpm — but the rhythm grid deliberately jitters
by ±15 ms on the 2 and 4 (applied post-Silero-VAD gating).

## Chord implication
A sections walk I-vi-ii-V (D-Bm-Em-A). B lifts to IV-V-iii-vi (G-A-F#m-Bm)
before the last A. `m.tin_pan_turnaround` is announced cleanly at the A-end
even as the click wanders.

## Production shape
- Vox (us1), tessitura 200-520 Hz — a little higher, a little playful.
- Ring-mod ghost carrier at 40 Hz, gated to match the wobble — rises and falls
  with the jitter.
- csdr ragtime stride kept dead-on-grid; the Praat-sourced metronome click
  is what wanders. The contrast is the whole gag.
- Klatt texture supplies a tiny glockenspiel-like ping on each A cadence.

## Pronunciation hints
- "metronome" as `'m E t r @ n oU m` — four syllables, `tr` kept light.
- "wobbles" as `'w A b @ l z` — soft `b`, open vowel.
- "mantel" as `'m & n t @ l`.
- Avoid "broken"'s `kn` cluster — voice it as `'b r oU k @ n` and taper the `n`.
- "maybe" as `'m eI b i`.

## Character
We are charmed by imperfection. The metronome is a friend, not a failure.
The B section reassures it. `m.hook` surfaces on the "Honey" couplets; we
reuse that "Honey" figure across the album as a unifying sweetener.
