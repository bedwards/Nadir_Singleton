# Lighthouse Lil — notes

Slow AABA in G major at 88 bpm — the "waltz of the sea" tempo, but in 4/4.
32 bars: A (8) A (8) B (8) A (8).

## Chord implication
A sections sit on I-vi7-ii7-V7 (G-Em7-Am7-D7). The bridge sweeps to IV-ii-V
(C-Am-D7) and returns. The `m.tin_pan_turnaround` lives on the last two bars
of each A: Am-D7-G.

## Production shape
- Vox (us3) lowered tessitura 160-480 Hz — Lil's voice is warmer, rounder.
- Ring-mod ghost at 40 Hz is slightly louder here to suggest the tower hum.
- csdr stride is brushed, not struck — we swap the Dirac for a filtered-noise
  shwish on off-beats.
- A held "oo" vowel on the final A bar, Praat-tuned to G, mimes the lamp beam.

## Pronunciation hints
- "lighthouse" as `'l aI t h aU s` — two separable syllables, space the stops.
- "lantern" as `'l & n t @ n` — skip any `rn` cluster temptation.
- "kettle" as `'k E t @ l` — small schwa, clean `t`.
- "melody" as `'m E l @ d i` — glide the `l`.
- We avoid "strength" and "twelfth" territory entirely.

## Character
Lil is patient, unhurried, keeping watch. The song is a thank-you card. `m.hook`
is the four-syllable "Lil and the ocean" motif that repeats at each A end.
