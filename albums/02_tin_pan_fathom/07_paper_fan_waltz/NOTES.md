# Paper Fan Waltz — notes

3/4 AABA in A major, 96 bpm. 48 bars = 4 x 12. A TPA waltz interlude.

## Chord implication
A sections: I-I-vi-vi-ii-ii-V-V-I-I-V-I (12 bars), a slow circular waltz.
B lifts to IV-IV-iv-iv-I-VI7-ii-V7-I-I-V-I. `m.tin_pan_turnaround`
articulates the last three bars of every A.

## Production shape
- Vox (us1), tessitura 190-500 Hz.
- Ring-mod ghost at 40 Hz is modulated by a 0.33 Hz sine — a slow wobble
  that mimics the fan's travel.
- csdr texture: noise brushed by a low-pass FIR, gated on beat 1, soft.
- Beat 2 and 3 carry no hit — we keep the space airy.
- Klatt texture: a gentle whistle for the "seven-fifteen" lyric.
