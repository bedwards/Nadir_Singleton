# Honey in the Reel — notes

AABA in Bb major at 100 bpm. 32 bars. A love letter to the silent-film
accompanist.

## Chord implication
A sections: I-I7-IV-iv-I-vi-ii-V7. That I7 into IV is the classic TPA
"cinema chord". B modulates briefly to III (D major) for eight bars, then
pivots back via F7 to Bb. `m.tin_pan_turnaround` caps each A as always.

## Production shape
- Vox (us1), tessitura 200-520 Hz.
- Ring-mod ghost at 40 Hz runs a slow 0.1 Hz amplitude drift — the ghost
  of a film-reel flicker.
- csdr stride is tack-piano flavoured: we pre-emphasise 2-4 kHz on the
  texture.
- Klatt texture supplies a very faint white-noise hiss under the whole
  track, envelope-gated to lyric peaks — the projector sound.
- A Praat-shaped impulse on "chase" for word-painting, three times.

## Character
Tender, amused, grateful to the unnamed working musician. `m.hook`
is the descending "Honey in the reel" on 5-4-3-1.
