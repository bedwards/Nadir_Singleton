# Goodnight, Tin Pan — notes

AABA in C major at 68 bpm. 32 bars. The album's closing curtain.

## Chord implication
A sections: I-vi-ii-V | I-IV-I-V7 — gentle, circular, plagal-coloured.
B: IV-iv-I-VI7-ii-V7-I-I. The final A closes on a long ritardando
`m.tin_pan_turnaround` that dissolves into a held C.

## Production shape
- Vox (us3), tessitura 160-440 Hz — the album's lowest, gentlest.
- Ring-mod ghost at 40 Hz fades in over the first eight bars and out over
  the last eight: the whole album palette waving goodnight.
- csdr stride is a whisper — one filtered noise-sweep per bar.
- Klatt texture calls back: the "moon" bell from track 06, the "bell"
  from track 13, the "chime" from track 15, each a single soft hit in
  the B section.
- The very last two bars leave vocal alone, no bed — the ghost has gone
  home.

## Character
A gentle farewell. Every verse lists characters and objects from earlier
tracks — we reward the listener for having stayed. `m.hook` is the
descending "Goodnight to the ___" figure on 3-2-1, repeated until the
last line resolves on the tonic.

## Album-level callback
This closer shares its `m.hook` contour with track 01's opener hook,
inverted; it feels like we went out and came back.
