# 03 The Index Withdraws — notes

## Descent step
Proper nouns, place names, time markers — all the indexicals that anchor speech to the world — are systematically removed. Only "we" remains as a referent. The song is grammatically thinning. We foreshadow the final disappearance of content words.

## Embedding of `m.nadir_silence`
The words "thing", "place", "hour" are each followed immediately by a 600 ms silence — the Morse dash width for track 14. Three dashes in a row encode nothing yet, but the dimension is being established.
