we folded them
on top of the book
the little hinges clicked
the way they always do
we were not finished reading
we were going to pick them up again
in ten minutes
it has been three days
the page remembers the sentence
we do not
which is perhaps the correct arrangement
a book should keep a reader
not the other way around
we will pick up the glasses
tomorrow
or the day after
and the page will be waiting
