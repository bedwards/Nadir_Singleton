the small hours
are not small
they are the longest hours
we have
they are spent
with all of these things
the window
the glove
the clock
the scale
the lamp
the page
the spoon
the letter
the kettle
the hook
the glasses
the moth
the thimble
the glass
the map
we have been their company
and they have been ours
and in a minute
the window will decide
what the morning will be
and we will agree again
