the last lamp
at the end of the street
is still lit
the others have gone
one by one
in the order we did not learn
we stand under it
because it is the one
that has not given up
we know it will
we know we will
but not yet
not yet
the filament holds
the hum is almost a note
the note is almost a vowel
the vowel is almost a word
and the word
is oh
