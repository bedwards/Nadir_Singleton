a paper map
of a city we have been to
we folded it wrong
the creases disagree
the city is there
but only if we ignore the folds
we have not unfolded it since
we have no plans to return
we keep the map
because we were there
and because paper forgets slower than we do
there is a coffee stain
on what we think is the river
we consider this appropriate
we put the map back in the drawer
with the thimble
with the hook of a key
we do not remember the lock for
