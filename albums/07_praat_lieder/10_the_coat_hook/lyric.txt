a brass hook
by the door
empty
it was not always empty
we know this
because it is worn
where the strap used to rest
a small wear-mark
shaped like patience
we have not taken a coat down from it
in a long time
we have not needed to
the hook keeps holding
what it no longer holds
which is a kind of holding
we stand near it
we brush it once with a knuckle
we go back inside
