Inside the canvas tent we listen low
A ratio of a hum and a little hollow wind
Clean on the evens, cloudy on the odd
We measure out the air between the bells

A little bit of harmony, a little bit of haze
Decibels of daylight, decibels of dark
We hold a note as narrow as a pin
And let the softer shadows settle in

Inside the canvas tent we listen late
A balance of a bell and a gentle breathing veil
Clean when we can, cloudy when we must
We carry every colour of the dust
