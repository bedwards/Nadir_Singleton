Three little cards of a velvet and a hum
F one is a whisper, F two is a drum
F three is a secret of a distant little bell
A reading in a resonance, a fortune she will tell

A vowel is a valley, a vowel is a hill
A shift of a formant is a turn of a will
We listen to the frequencies the little candle flings
And follow every answer that a careful vowel brings

Three little cards on a velvet little tray
The teller holds a tone, and the tone begins to sway
A whisper, and a drum, and a distant little bell
We thank her for the fortune and we wave and say farewell
