# 14 Hold — notes

## Why this slot
Closing. We resolve the album back to A minor (matching track 01's key) but at the lowest tempo of the record, 54 bpm, in the lower half of the tessitura. The singleton is not asleep; it is holding. The final word "morning" is seeded for album 02, which inherits the dawn-voicing palette.

## Form and motif deployment
A minor (Aeolian), 54 bpm, 40 bars — the longest track on the album. `m.dawn_utterance` is inverted for the last time: plateau first (bars 1–24 on A3, unvarying), then a descending minor third (A3 to F3) over the final sixteen bars. The album closes on F3, not the tonic — a small, deliberate unresolve, so the next record has a place to begin.
